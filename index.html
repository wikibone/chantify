🕉️ Sai Gayathri Mantra

📜 Lyrics

Om Sayeeshwaraya Vidhmahe
Sathya Devaya Dheemahi
Thannah Sarvah Prachodayaat

✨ Meaning

“We meditate on Lord Sai, who embodies Truth and Divinity. May that Supreme Being enlighten our intellect and guide us along the path of righteousness.”
